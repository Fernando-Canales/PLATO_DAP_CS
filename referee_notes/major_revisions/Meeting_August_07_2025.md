Diagnostic Check for 24 vs 6 Camera Comparison:
1. Calculate efficiency difference: Δeff = eff_24cam - eff_6cam
2. Calculate combined uncertainty: σ_combined = √(σ_24cam² + σ_6cam²) 
3. Test significance: |Δeff| / σ_combined > 3 → statistically significant

The diagnostic shows that at P = 10.5 and P = 11.5 the secondary flux has a significant difference between 24 and 6 cameras. This also holds for Secondary centroid shifts.
